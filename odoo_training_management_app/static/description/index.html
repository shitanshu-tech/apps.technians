Employee Training Management Application

Training Management for Employees Odoo App

This app allows your company to conduct or organized training for your employees.



Main Features
  • Training: Allow you to create and manage the training of your employees.
  • Training/Applications: Your employees or training team can create a training application request.
  • Training/Employee Training: Employee training will be created with course and subjects.
  • Training/Tasks: For every course and subject system will create tasks for employees.
  • Training/Trainers: Trainers will be listed here.
  • Training/Courses and Subjects: You can configure the course, subject, and class rooms.
  • Training/Configuration/Application Stages: This is configurable so you can manage stages.
  • Training/Configuration/Employee Training Stages: This is configurable so you can manage stages as per your training process.
  • Training/Application Report: As shown in below sample.
  • For more details please watch video and check below screenshots.
